Macaroni and Cheese

Description

This devine lasagna recipe is the perfect dish for any occassion.
It is also very simple to make with only 5 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 500g mince meat
  • 300g of Lasagna sheets
  • Dolmio Tomato Sauce or follow our homemade recipe here
  • Dolmio Cream Sauce or follow our homemade recipe here
  • 125g grated cheese for topping

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tbsp of oilive oil in a pan and then add the 500g of mince in
    two batches for roughly 10minutes until browned all over.

  2. Pour the tomato sauce over the mince and mix well.
    Leave to simmer for 10 mins or until the sauce looks rich.

  3. Heat the oven to 180C / 160C fan / gas 4. Add a layer of lasagna
    sheets to the bottom of an ovenproof dish, and then spoon about
    a third of the meat on top and drizzle over about 130g of the
    white cream sauce.

  4. Repeat step 3 until you have three layers of pasta.
    pour the remaining white sauce on top and sprinkle over
    the grated cheese.

  5. Place the lasagna in the oven to bake for 45mins until
    the top is bubbly and brown.
