Ingredients

  • Pot Noodle any flavour of your choice but we've gone with the classic chicken and mushroom :)
  • Boiled water

Steps

  1. Boil water in a kettle or on a gas hob.

  2. Peel back the foil lid about halfway and remove sachet.
    Pour the water up to the line indicated.

  3. Cover the pot with the foil lid and leave for
    2 - 3mins.

  4. Stir in the sachet contents and leave for another
    2 minutes.
